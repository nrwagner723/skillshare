About Armin

Armin is an anime character from the popular show Attack on Titan. He is very intelligent and brave. He will do whatever it takes to help his friends.

Armin and his friends

Armin's Characteristics

  1. Armin loves the beach. It was his dream to go there one day and he finally did.
  2. He's one of the top 5 smartest people in his academics class.
  3. He doesn't have high self-esteem but is determined to prove his worth to himself and the world.

Armin's Friends
